Projects

Indoor human activity monitoring in Smart home

We design a system which uses cameras and geophones to detect and recognize human activity. The data is video data and vibration data. We use representation learning method to extract feature and trying different model to make classification. This system can help the older people live better if they are living alone in a single house.

Real-time Ambient Noise Seismic Imaging

This project creates a real-time Ambient Noise Seismic Imaging system, to study and monitor the subsurface sustainability and potential hazards of geological structures. Understanding and addressing the subsurface sustainability has significant impact on the natural, social, and economic issues of the region and across the globe. The system is comprised of a self-sustainable sensor network of geophones that can autonomously perform in-network computing of the 3D shallow earth structure images based on ambient noise alone.

Cough events detection

We Developed a fog computing based system to monitoring the cough events with smartphone. A smartphone-based app which including segmentation function and MFCC-HMM method is developed to gathering data. A CNN based classification method is running on cloud to get high specificity.

Beddot Product

A contactless and privacy-preserving sensor device, attached to frame underneath a bed, monitors sleep activities, including time-to-bed, timeout-of-bed, number and duration of bed exits, bed restlessness, posture change and urgent events, and estimates heart rate and respiration rate.

Multi-object Tracking in Videos

A framework of multi-object tracking in videos was proposed and implemented from base models, the RetinaNet and the MemTrack network.
